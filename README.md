# 📊 Campfly Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

The Campfly Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, product profitability, customer behavior, regional performance, and business growth trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Addressed

- Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which sales channels contribute the most to overall revenue?
- Which customers drive the highest sales performance?
- Which cities and regions perform best and worst?
- How do sales and profits trend over time?
- What are the key growth patterns across different channels?

---

## 📂 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit

- Order Quantity

- Revenue Distribution by Cities

### 2️⃣ Product Performance Dashboard
- Top Products by Revenue
- Top Products by Profit

- Product Demand Analysis
- Product Performance Insights

### 3️⃣ Channel & Regional Analysis Dashboard
- Revenue by Channel
- Profit by Channel
- Revenue Share by City
- Top & Bottom Performing Cities
- Geographic Sales Distribution

### 4️⃣ Sales Trends & Growth Dashboard
- Annual Revenue Performance
- Channel Growth Trends
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Monthly Profit Trends
- Business Growth Analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- **Total Revenue:** 154.57M
- **Total Profit:** 143.13M
- **Profit Margin:** 92.76%
- Wholesale channel contributed the highest revenue.
- Product 7 emerged as the top-performing product.
- March recorded the highest monthly revenue.
